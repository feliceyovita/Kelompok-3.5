Simonang-Monang Waterfall

Area

Rating

Deskripsi

Simonang-Monang Waterfall is located in Desa Bandar Pulau Pekan, Kecamatan Bandar Pulau, approximately 73 km from Kisaran City. It is a hidden gem in North Sumatra, offering a serene and natural atmosphere. Known for its pristine waters and lush surroundings, Simonang-Monang is a perfect destination for those seeking tranquility in nature.
